Rough idea of university library DB schema


patrons
---------
patron_id PK
email
date created
university_id

librarians
----------
librarian_id PK
email
date created
university_id

books
---------
book_id PK
title
author
call number
publisher
publication year
location in library
# of copies
current status
keywords
coverage image

checkoutList
---------
checkout_id PK
issued_date
due_date
fine_amount
patron_id FK
book_id FK

waitingList
----------
waiting_id PK
date requested
checkout status
patron_id FK
book_id FK
